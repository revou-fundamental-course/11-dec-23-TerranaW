About

Moena High School is 3-year a public high school in Jakarta, Indonesia. It is located on Jalan Apel, Jakarta. The school received Accreditation ‘A’ from National Accreditation Board (BAN). Founded in August 1, 1958, Moena High School is well known for its highly competitive admission process, achievements in science competitions such as the National Science Olympiad, and an excellent performance of its students in the Indonesian public university entrance examination. The school has been ranked as one of the best high schools in the country for multiple times, and recently ranked 4th nationally and 2nd highest in Jakarta province based on the average score on National University Placement Test (UTBK) 2020. Many of its students study in University of Indonesia and Bandung Institute of Technology, considered Indonesia's most prestigious colleges along with Gadjah Mada University, as well as universities abroad, many of them pursue degrees in medicine, engineering and economics.

Vision

Creating high quality graduates in the aspects of personality, academics and non-academics of international standard

Mission

  • Organizing learning activities that are interactive, inspirational, fun, challenging students to participate actively and creatively.
  • Organizing a learning system that encourages actualization of student competencies.
  • Carrying out personality development activities that include faith and noble morals.
  • Organizing interest and talent development activities based on needs and achievement oriented.
  • Implement efficient, effective, transparent and accountable school governance.
